How to Walk a Dog

This page describes how to walk a dog.

Ingredients

  • a dog
  • a human (you)
  • a leash
  • a collar or harness
  • a sunny day

Steps

  1. Put the collar or harness on the dog
  2. Attach the leash to the dog
  3. While holding the leash, giving enough slack to the dog, walk around and enjoy the sunny day
